Around the world

Finally here is my last chapter. In this chapter, I will talk about some of my personal experiences of being in another country:

  • In my life, I have been to over 15 countries and have been all over the world.

  • First I want to talk about my trip to New York this summer. We travelled to New York on a bus with some friends. It took around 10 hours to get there. The trip there was fun as we took breaks like every hour or so. I also brought some toys and other stuff to keep me entertained during the trip. In New York, we travelled around the city and saw many sites such as; the U.N headquarters, the 9/11 memorial and museum, Empire State Building, Grand Central Station, Statue of Liberty and Times Square. These sites in New York were Awesome!

  • Second I want to talk about my trips to Europe. I have been to many countries in Europe and now I want to talk about some of my Experiences there. I remember when my family and I visited London, England we went to a lot of museums such as the Natural History Museum and the Science Museum. I really liked the Natural History Museum because they had fossils of ancient creatures also they had animatronic dinosaurs. Those were really cool and I thought they were real dinosaurs because they looked so natural and alive. Also when we went to Germany we visited a concentration camp. This camp was scary to me back then as I remember looking through a window and seeing a room full of human skulls. We also saw videos of life in the camp which looked horrible and inhumane. That was one experience in my life that I will never forget.

  • Another time we went to South Africa and we went to an African Lion Safari and saw real animals. It was really cool. We went to a caged vehicle and saw lions, cheetahs, buffalo and other animals. I remember one of the lions came right in front of the door of the van and just stared at us for a minute or so. That moment was really scary as I thought the lion would break in the van. We also saw penguins in South Africa. They were really small and cool.

Also after that was over then when you attempted to put your belt established on than the hot metal would burn and sting your hands as the metal of the seat belt absorbed a lot of the heat trapped in the car.
