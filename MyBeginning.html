My Beginning

In the beginning

Moreover, this section is about my life as a preschool child and today I will tell you some of my experiences and stories.

  • First of all, I remember when I was younger I wanted to be a spy because when I was young, I really liked Tom Cruise and his movie series “Mission Impossible”. As I grew older, I began to take an interest in other things, such as technology and books, and began to despise the idea of becoming a spy. Now, today, I want to be a programmer or a piano teacher.

  • Secondly, I really did not like school when I was younger, as I remember times where I would cry just to get out of that place. When I was in Junior Kindergarten in Riyadh, I remember when my dad would drop me off and then when he left, I would run to the door and the teacher would have to stop me from escaping from the classroom. Also, I remember a time when I ran for the door, but for some reason, I knocked a board game off a shelf and my teacher got really mad at me, and she had to put me on the floor.

  • These are some of my experiences in school. Also, when I was young, I had developed a phobia of spiders and bugs. Since we lived in Riyadh, there were a lot of bugs and I would always be scared.

School days, dear old schools days

Moving on, in this section we will talk about school days..

  • First of all, I want to talk about my feeling about school. In my first day of school, I often feel very nervous and scared as the new school year arrives. I often meet some familiar faces on the first day of school. These faces often become future friends. School can be very stressful especially on the first day.

  • Today I want to talk about my first day of middle school. On my first day, I remember approaching the red building that will be my future school. I walked in as I saw some familiar faces from my school. The air was filled with nervousness and a feeling of fear as I approached my new school. I began to sit down and wait for my name to be called. Finally, after waiting for minutes which felt like hours, my name was finally called. I walked into my classroom and saw my new teacher whom I would have for 3 years. This was the beginning of a long journey through middle school.

  • Next, I wannt to talk about my future in school. I am worried that school will become harder as we will get more pressure to make more important decisions in our young lives. Also, I am really scared and excited about high school to begin. I am nervous that I will do bad in high school and will not make the right decisions. I am also excited to face my upcoming future as I find this as an opportunity to learn new things and meet new people like me. I am excited in knowing that we will have more freedom in making our decisions in things such as course selection. One course I am really excited for is the Computer Science course in Rick Hansen as I love computers!
